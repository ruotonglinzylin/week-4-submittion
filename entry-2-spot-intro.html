LONG ISLAND CITY COURTHOUSE
2510 Ct Square W, Queens, NY 11101
nycourts.gov
80/100 recommended

In 1976, the structure earned recognition as a New York City Landmark, and it also holds a place on the New York State and National Registers of Historic Places. This courthouse has been witness to significant trials, such as the infamous 1927 murder trial of Ruth Snyder and her paramour, Henry Judd Gray.
